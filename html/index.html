Hi there!

My name is Jeanie Jung: A Korean-blooded Filipino-hearted sophomore at UC Berkeley, majoring in Computer Science. Having been born in Korea and raised in China and the Philippines has shaped me into a creative, curious and passionate individual with a burning love for new explorations. Besides computer programming, I love to dance, play the flute, travel, read inspirational books, and take pictures of food and other beautiful things.

Interests

TRAVEL

Having been raised in a family of explorers, I have travelled to more than 20 countres and still counting! :) I think there is nothing more exciting than learning about other people's cultures and beliefs, and of course, their cuisine! Some countries I wish to explore are Spain, Mexico, and Vietnam!

FLUTE

The first time I held the flute was when I was ten years old. Ever since then I fell in love with music and explored several instruments. I stayed with playing the flute and traveled abroad to play in several international concert bands, playing under amazing conductors including Robert W. Smith and Jerry Luckhardt! Currently, I am studying Mozart's Flute Concerto No. 1.

DANCE

I began dancing at the age of 8 with tap dancing. By 14, I was also doing hip hop, jazz, figure skating, ballet, and more. I especially loved contemporary dance in high school and was privileged to represent my school in Korea, Japan, and China. In college, my interest shifted to hip hop. I am currently an alumni of AFX Competitive Team and a member of Identity X. My favorite choreographer is Sorah Yang and my favorite dance team is Cookies.

SUDOKU

If you see me around, you'll probably find my eyes fastened onto my phone. I was in Grade 3 when my brother introduced me to the world of sudoku. I immediately fell in love with its mystery, memorization, and logic, and subscribed myself to the monthly Sudoku magazines.
